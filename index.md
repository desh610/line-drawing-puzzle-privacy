# Privacy Policy

**App Name:** Line Drawing Puzzle  
**Developer:** RD Projects  
**Last Updated:** June 2026  

---

## Overview

Line Drawing Puzzle is a puzzle game developed by RD Projects. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data when you use our app.

---

## Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information directly from you.

Anonymous data may be collected to improve gameplay experience, such as level completion rates and crash reports, through Google Play Services.

---

## Permissions We Use

Our app requests the following permissions:

**INTERNET**
Required to display advertisements via Google AdMob and to process in-app purchases via Google Play Billing.

**VIBRATE**
Used to provide haptic feedback when you connect nodes in a puzzle. No data is collected through this permission.

**POST_NOTIFICATIONS**
Used only to send you a daily reminder notification to play the game. Notifications are scheduled locally on your device. No data is sent to any server. You can disable this at any time in Settings → Notifications or inside the app under Settings → Daily Reminder.

**BILLING**
Required to process in-app purchases securely through Google Play. We never see or store your payment information - all transactions are handled entirely by Google Play.

**ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE**
Used to check your internet connection before attempting to process in-app purchases, so we can show you a helpful message if you're offline instead of letting the purchase fail silently.

## Third-Party Services

**Google AdMob**
- Displays advertisements in the app
- May collect device identifiers for ad personalisation
- You can opt out via: Device Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation
- AdMob Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

**Google Play Billing**
- Handles all in-app purchase transactions securely
- We never see or store your payment information
- Google Play Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

**Google Play Services**
- Used for app distribution, updates, and crash reporting

---

## Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device only and is never sent to us:

- Puzzle progress, stars earned, and unlocked levels
- Coin balance
- Sound and vibration preferences
- Notification settings

---

## In-App Purchases

This app offers optional in-app purchases including coin packs and an ad removal option. All purchases are processed securely through Google Play. Coins are virtual currency with no real-world monetary value and are non-refundable unless required by applicable law.

---

## Advertisements

This app displays ads via Google AdMob. Ads may be personalised based on your interests. To opt out of personalised advertising, go to your device **Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation**.
